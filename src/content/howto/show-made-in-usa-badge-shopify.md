## Compliance note

Made In USA has legal definitions per the FTC. Do not apply the badge to products that do not meet the "all or virtually all" standard, or you risk FTC action.

## Step 1. Audit your products

Identify which products qualify as Made In USA per FTC definition.

## Step 2. Tag qualifying products

Add the tag "origin-usa" to each qualifying product.

## Step 3. Create the badge in Made In

Map the "origin-usa" tag to the USA flag. Customize the label if you want "American Made" or similar.

## Step 4. Display

The app renders the badge on every tagged product automatically.

## Step 5. Collection page display

Turn on the collection page option to show the badge on product cards in collections.
