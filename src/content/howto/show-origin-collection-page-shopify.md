## Why this matters

Collection pages are where shoppers browse. Origin badges on tiles reduce friction for shoppers who specifically want products from a certain country.

## Step 1. Enable collection display

In Made In settings, toggle on collection page display.

## Step 2. Place the app block

In the theme editor, navigate to the collection template. Add the Made In collection block to the product card area.

## Step 3. Size the badge

Collection page badges should be smaller than product page badges. Default 16px works for most themes.

## Step 4. Position

Top-right of the product tile is conventional. Top-left works if your theme has a Sale badge already in the top-right.
