## The concept

A live countdown "order in 2h 13m for delivery Tuesday" creates urgency and reinforces the ETA.

## Step 1. Enable the countdown in Arrively

Inside the app settings, turn on the countdown option. Pick the copy pattern: "Order in X for Y delivery".

## Step 2. Set the cut-off time

Same as your dispatch cut-off. Typical values: 12pm, 2pm, 4pm.

## Step 3. Test across time zones

Shopify shows the cut-off in the shopper's local time, not your warehouse time. Verify this logic works if you operate across zones.

## Step 4. Place the widget

The countdown renders inside the Arrively ETA block. Position the block just below add-to-cart where it gets maximum attention.

## Step 5. Monitor

Check your cart-to-checkout conversion rate for the week after adding the countdown. A 5-10 percent lift is typical.
