## When custom badges make sense

Your brand has specific callouts that do not map to standard labels. Examples: "Handmade", "Vegan", "Last Chance", "Staff Pick".

## Step 1. Plan your copy

Custom badges work best with short copy, 1 to 3 words. "Handmade" yes, "Hand-crafted in Vermont" no.

## Step 2. Pick colors

Your brand primary color for aspirational badges (Staff Pick), red for urgency (Last Chance), green for certifications (Vegan).

## Step 3. Create in Product Badges

Add badge, enter copy, pick color, pick shape (pill works for most), save.

## Step 4. Target

Tag-based targeting is simplest. Tag products with "vegan", "handmade", "staff-pick" as needed, then target those tags.

## Step 5. Design tips

Keep badges contrasted with your product image background. Test on mobile where badge area is smaller.
