## The CSV format

Three required columns: email, amount, note (optional). Example: "customer@email.com,25.00,Thanks for your patience with the shipping delay". One row per customer.

## Step 1. Prepare your CSV

Export the target customer segment from Shopify admin as CSV. Add an amount column. Add a note column if you want a custom message per customer.

## Step 2. Upload

In the Bulk Store Credit dashboard, go to Bulk Import, upload the CSV.

## Step 3. Preview

The app shows a preview: N customers, total $X issued, any errors like invalid email or missing Shopify customer record.

## Step 4. Confirm

Click Import. The app issues credit to each customer and sends notification emails. Failed rows are logged for retry.

## Step 5. Download receipt

After import, download a receipt CSV showing every success and failure. Keep this for accounting.

## Step 6. Notify customers

Optional: send a separate campaign email via Klaviyo or Shopify Email letting customers know they got credit and how to use it.
