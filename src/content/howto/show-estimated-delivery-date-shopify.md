## Why this matters

Shoppers abandon carts when delivery timing is unclear. Showing ETA on the product page lifts conversion 5-15 percent.

## Step 1. Install Arrively

From the Shopify App Store. The app adds a theme app block to your product page template.

## Step 2. Turn on the app block

Open the theme editor, navigate to the product template, add the Arrively ETA block just below the add-to-cart button.

## Step 3. Set your dispatch cut-off

In the Arrively dashboard, set the time you stop processing orders each day. Example: "orders placed before 2pm ship same day".

## Step 4. Set shipping zones

Add zones for your primary markets. Example: US zone 1 = 2 days, US zone 2 = 4 days, Canada = 7 days, EU = 10 days.

## Step 5. Verify

Open a product on your storefront. Confirm the ETA appears and the date makes sense based on today's date.

## Step 6. Tune

Watch the first week of orders. If actual delivery is later than the ETA predicts, extend your zone windows.
