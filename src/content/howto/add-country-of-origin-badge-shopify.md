## Why origin matters

Shoppers for premium apparel, food, and crafts pay more when origin is clear. Shopify does not display origin natively.

## Step 1. Install Made In

From the Shopify App Store. The app adds a theme app block for product and collection pages.

## Step 2. Tag your products

In Shopify admin, add tags like "origin-usa", "origin-italy", "origin-japan" to each product.

## Step 3. Map tags in Made In

Open the Made In dashboard and map each tag to a country. The app pulls the flag automatically.

## Step 4. Turn on the app block

In the theme editor, add the Made In block to your product template. Position near the product title.

## Step 5. Customize

Pick flag size, label style ("Made In", "Crafted In", custom), and placement.

## Step 6. Verify

Open a tagged product and confirm the badge shows correctly.
