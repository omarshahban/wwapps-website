## Use case

You want a badge to appear on every product in a specific collection, without tagging each product individually.

## Step 1. Create the collection

In Shopify admin, create an automated or manual collection. Example: "Summer Sale 2026".

## Step 2. Create the badge

In Product Badges, add a badge with the campaign copy.

## Step 3. Collection targeting

In the badge rules, choose "Target by collection" and pick the Summer Sale collection.

## Step 4. Set start and end dates

Pro plan: schedule the badge to auto-show on the campaign start date and auto-remove on the end date. Free plan: toggle on and off manually.

## Step 5. Verify

Check products inside the collection (badge shows) and outside (badge hidden).
