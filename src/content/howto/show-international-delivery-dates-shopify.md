## The principle

Do not show a single international ETA. Different countries have very different transit times. Segment by country.

## Step 1. Identify your top international markets

From Shopify analytics, find the countries that represent more than 2 percent of international revenue.

## Step 2. Research transit times

Check your carrier's published transit times for each. Add 1-2 days of buffer for customs.

## Step 3. Build the zones

In Arrively, create separate zones per country or region. Assign accurate transit windows.

## Step 4. Display

The app auto-detects the visitor's country and shows the appropriate ETA. No action needed after setup.

## Step 5. Customs messaging

For destinations with customs, add a note: "Delivery time includes customs clearance which may vary".
