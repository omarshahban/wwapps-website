## Three scenarios

You want to: turn off a specific badge, hide a badge on one product, or remove badges entirely.

## Turn off a specific badge

In Product Badges dashboard, toggle the badge to inactive. It stops rendering on all products.

## Hide a badge on one product

Two options. Remove the targeting tag from that product, or add an exclusion rule: "Show on collection X except product Y".

## Remove all badges

Uninstall the Product Badges app. Because the app uses theme app extensions, there is no leftover code in your theme.

## Before you uninstall

Export your badge config from the dashboard if you might reinstall later.
