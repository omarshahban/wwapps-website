## Setup

Made In ships with 200+ country flags. Each one displays as a clean SVG, not emoji, so the design stays consistent across themes.

## Step 1. Install and tag

Follow the country-of-origin badge setup. Tag products with country codes.

## Step 2. Pick flag style

Rectangle, circle, or rounded. Pick the one that matches your theme.

## Step 3. Place the app block

In the theme editor, place the Made In app block. Try above the title, below the title, or inline with the price.

## Step 4. Size

Default flag size is 24px. Adjust up or down based on your layout.
