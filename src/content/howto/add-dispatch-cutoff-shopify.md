## Why this works

Explicit cut-off removes ambiguity. "Order by 2pm for same-day dispatch" is a clearer value than "fast shipping".

## Step 1. Decide the cut-off

Match it to your actual warehouse cut-off. Lying here creates angry customers.

## Step 2. Set in Arrively

Configure the cut-off time in the dashboard. The app automatically updates the message based on current time.

## Step 3. Customize the copy

Default: "Order in 2h 13m for same-day dispatch". Customize to match your brand voice.

## Step 4. Test across devices

On mobile, the cut-off message can get cut off if your theme has tight product page layout. Test and adjust.

## Step 5. Monitor for accuracy

Watch the first week. If orders close to cut-off are not shipping same-day, move the cut-off earlier.
