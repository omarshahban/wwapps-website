## How we approached this comparison

We ran both apps side by side on the same Dawn theme test store for a week. Same products, same collections, same visitor traffic. We measured storefront render, checked the admin workflow for creating a "Sale" and a "New" badge, and tried to target a badge at a specific collection.

## Where Win-Win wins

The free tier is the biggest gap. Badgify's free trial expires, and any real use of the app requires at least the $9.99 tier. Our free plan gives you 2 active badges on all themes, forever. For unlimited badges, Pro is $6.99 per month, still well under Badgify's entry paid tier.

Our Pro tier is cheaper than Badgify's entry paid tier, with no per-tier feature gating beyond the badge count.

## Where Badgify wins

Badgify has been around longer and has a wider library of pre-built badge designs. If you want a pre-built "Limited Edition" badge with a specific retro style, they probably already have it. We ship cleaner defaults and expect you to tweak colors from there.

Badgify's review count is also much bigger, which helps if social proof matters to you when picking apps.

## Which one should you pick

If you are a new or small store and money matters, pick us. If you are a larger brand with a specific template requirement and want to lean on a mature vendor with thousands of reviews, pick Badgify. For most merchants in between, the free tier and lower paid price is the decisive factor.
