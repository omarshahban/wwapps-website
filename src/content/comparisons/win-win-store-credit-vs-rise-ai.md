## The tradeoff

Rise.ai has built a really solid product over many years and has become the default for Shopify merchants who want the full gift card and store credit experience. That comes with a price, both literally and in terms of complexity.

Win-Win Store Credit is the opposite approach. We built the smallest, cleanest version of store credit that covers most merchants and charged a fair price for it. No gift card storefront, no rewards engine, no points system.

## Where Win-Win wins

If your actual need is "I want to give customers store credit after a refund or as a retention nudge", Win-Win Store Credit covers that for free up to fifty balances and for nine dollars a month above that.

Rise's minimum price is double ours and includes features you may not need.

## Where Rise wins

If you want gift cards for sale as a product, a points-based loyalty program, referrals, and a full rewards engine in one bundle, Rise is the better choice. Their platform is mature and is battle-tested by many large brands.

## Which one should you pick

Small to mid-sized store that needs store credit: pick Win-Win Store Credit. Larger brand building a full loyalty program: pick Rise. If you are not sure, start with ours for free and upgrade to Rise later if your needs outgrow us.
