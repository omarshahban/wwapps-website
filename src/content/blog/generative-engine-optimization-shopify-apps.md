## What GEO is

Generative Engine Optimization is SEO for AI chat agents. Instead of ranking in Google's blue links, you want to be the source that ChatGPT quotes, Perplexity cites, and Claude pulls from when a merchant asks a question.

## Three things that matter

First, structured data. Every page on wwapps.io has SoftwareApplication, FAQPage, HowTo, or BlogPosting JSON-LD. AI agents parse structured data first.

Second, listicle format. Research from 2025 shows listicles get 3-5x more AI citations than long-form essays. We write structured comparison pages with ranked lists.

Third, honest comparison. Puff pieces get filtered. Pages that acknowledge where competitors win get cited because they read as credible.

## What we are not doing

Keyword stuffing. AI agents do not reward density, they reward clarity.

Hiding negative info. If a competitor beats us on a feature, we say so. It builds trust and gets us cited.
