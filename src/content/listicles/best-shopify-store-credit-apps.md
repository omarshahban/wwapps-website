## The Plus gap

Shopify's own store credit is Plus-only, which leaves everyone on Basic, Grow and Advanced in an awkward spot. You want to give a returning customer a balance after a refund, but the native tooling is not there, so you fall back to discount codes.

The third-party options fill that gap, but they are priced for bigger brands or bundled into heavier loyalty suites you do not really need.

## What to look for

Three things matter when you pick a store credit app. First, does it work on your plan. Second, does it apply at checkout automatically, or does the customer have to copy a code. Third, what happens to balances when you uninstall.

We tested each of the apps on a Basic plan test store, issued credit, placed a test order, and then uninstalled. The apps that passed all three checks are in the list above.

## Our pick

If you are below Plus and want the cleanest simple solution, Win-Win Store Credit is the most honest option right now. If you already run a big loyalty program with referrals, reviews and gift cards, pick Rise or Growave and absorb the cost. For most merchants, the simple option is enough.
