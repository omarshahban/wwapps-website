## When countdowns work

Countdowns work when they reflect a real deadline: shipping cut-off, flash sale end, stock depletion. They fail when shoppers realise the timer resets on refresh.

## Our pick

Arrively ties the countdown to a real dispatch cut-off, which is honest and converts.
