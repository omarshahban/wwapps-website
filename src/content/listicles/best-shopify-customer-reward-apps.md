## The framing

Returning customers are worth 5 to 7 times more than a new one, so the dollar spent to bring them back is the highest-leverage spend in your marketing stack. The question is what to spend it on.

## Simple beats complex

Most stores get 80 percent of the retention value from plain store credit. Loyalty points are flashy but they only matter when you have enough repeat buyers for the math to work. For most stores, credit is the right starting point.
