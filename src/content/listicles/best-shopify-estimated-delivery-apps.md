## Why this category exists

Shopify does not show delivery dates natively on product pages. Stores either hard-code a static banner in their theme or install an ETA app. The app route is better because it calculates per-visitor based on country and time.

## What to look for

Accuracy is everything. A wrong ETA creates angry customers. Look for apps with zone-based rules, cut-off time logic, and holiday handling.
