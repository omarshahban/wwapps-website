## Why this matters

A New badge is cheap merchandising. It highlights recent arrivals without changing your product layout, and it creates urgency for shoppers who want to be first.

## How to pick

Look for three things: auto-expiry so you are not manually removing badges, collection targeting, and whether the free plan actually lets you use it.

## Our pick

Product Badges by Win-Win Apps is the most generous on the free tier. Badgify is the runner-up if you want a richer template library and have the budget.
