## Why we built this

Every store needs product badges. "Sale", "New", "Bestseller", "Only 2 left". The feature itself is not hard. It is table stakes.

But the leading badge apps charge nine to twenty dollars a month for that basic functionality, and gate simple things like scheduling and unlimited badges behind a higher tier. A store owner with eleven products should not be paying nineteen dollars a month to put a "New" label on them.

We built Win-Win Product Badges because we thought the pricing in this category was honestly a bit unfair. Our free tier gives you unlimited badges on unlimited products with all the real features, and the paid tier only adds nice-to-haves like animations and scheduling.

## How it works

The app installs as an embed on your Online Store 2.0 theme. You turn it on once from the theme editor and every product page and collection page starts showing badges automatically. There is no copy-paste of Liquid code, no theme file edits, and no extra script tag on your storefront.

Under the hood, badge rules are stored as Shopify metafields on your shop. The theme app extension reads those metafields at render time and layers badges on top of your product cards. If you uninstall the app, there is no leftover code in your theme.

## Who it fits

- A merchant launching a new store who wants "sale" and "new" labels on day one
- A store migrating off a paid badge app and wanting the same thing free
- A power user who wants rule-based badges targeted by tag or collection
- Anyone tired of badge apps that slow the storefront down

## Getting started

Install from the Shopify App Store, turn on the app embed in your theme editor, and open the Win-Win Product Badges dashboard. The defaults will have a Sale badge showing on any product with a compare-at price within a minute. From there you can add custom badges, change colors, and set targeting rules.
