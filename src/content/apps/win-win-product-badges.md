## Why we built this

Every product on your store looks the same to a shopper. A well-placed badge changes that. It instantly draws attention to your best deals, newest arrivals, and top sellers.

We built WW Badges to be the simplest, most flexible badge app on the Shopify App Store. No confusing setup, no developer needed. Design your badge, pick your target products, go live.

## How it works

The app installs as an embed on your Online Store 2.0 theme. Turn it on once from the theme editor and product pages start showing badges automatically. No copy-paste of liquid, no theme file edits, no extra script tag on your storefront.

Badge rules live as metafields on your shop. Uninstall the app and there is no leftover code in your theme.
