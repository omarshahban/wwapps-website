## The problem

Customers abandon carts when they cannot figure out when something will arrive. Most Shopify stores hide their shipping times deep in a footer policy page or on checkout, which is too late.

Arrively solves that by showing a clear, accurate estimated delivery date right on every product page, before the shopper even hits add-to-cart.

## How it works

Set your dispatch cut-off time and your shipping zone rules once. Arrively calculates the expected arrival date for each visitor based on their country and the current time, then shows it on the product page.

The app uses a theme app block so install is one click, and the message is fully customizable.
