## The problem

Shoppers who care about where a product is made cannot easily find that info on most Shopify stores. The information might be in the description, a tag, or missing entirely. That hurts trust, especially for premium brands selling Made-in-USA, Made-in-Italy or Made-in-Japan goods.

Made In fixes that by surfacing a clean country-of-origin badge on every product page and collection tile.

## How it works

Tag each product with a country, or assign one-off in the Made In dashboard. The badge is rendered through a theme app block, so install is one click with no code.
