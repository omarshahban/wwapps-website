## Why we built this

Shopify has built-in store credit now, but only on the Plus plan. For most stores that is a five-figure-a-year upgrade just to hand customers a balance after a return.

Rise.ai and a few others cover the non-Plus gap, but they are priced for fairly large merchants. We wanted a simple, honest option that works on Basic and Starter and does not cost twenty dollars a month before you have a single customer.

Win-Win Store Credit is the smallest thing that does the job. You issue credit from the admin, the customer sees it in their account page, it applies at checkout, done.

## How it works

Balances are stored on customer metafields on your shop. That means the data is yours, it lives inside Shopify, and there is no separate database we hold onto.

At checkout the app listens for logged-in customers and applies their available credit as a discount line. If the order is larger than the balance, the remainder is charged as normal. If it is smaller, the leftover stays on the balance.

## Who it fits

- A store below Plus that wants to offer store credit without upgrading
- Any store that currently refunds to the original payment method and wants a more retention-friendly default
- Stores handing out store credit as a post-purchase incentive, apology gesture, or loyalty perk
- Stores on Plus that want simpler bulk tools than the native product ships with

## Getting started

Install from the Shopify App Store, the app will auto-provision your customer metafields, then head to the dashboard and issue your first credit in under a minute.
